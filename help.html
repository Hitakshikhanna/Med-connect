📞 National Helplines (India)

102 — Ambulance (Government)

108 — Disaster Management / Emergency

112 — All-in-One Emergency Number

101 — Fire Station

100 — Police Control Room

1098 — Child Help Line

181 — Women Helpline

1075 — National Health Helpline


Quick First-Aid:
  • Ambulance: Call 102 or 108 immediately.
  • Heart Attack Signs: Look for chest pain, sweating, and shortness of breath.
  • Stroke (B.E. F.A.S.T.): Check Balance, Eyes, Face, Arms, and Speech.
  • Poison Control: Call 1800-116-117. Do not give water or milk.
  • Snake Bite: Keep the person still. Do not try to suck the venom out.
  • Mental Health Support: Call 1800-599-0019 (KIRAN Helpline).
  • Burn: Run cool (not cold) water over the area for 10-20 minutes.
  • Fainting: Lay the person on their back and lift their legs up.
  • Severe Bleeding: Apply firm, direct pressure with a clean cloth. Do not remove the cloth if it gets soaked; just add another one on top.
  • Dehydration: Drink ORS (Oral Rehydration Salts) or a mix of water, salt, and sugar. Sip slowly, do not gulp.

Tip: Always stay calm and speak clearly when calling helplines.
